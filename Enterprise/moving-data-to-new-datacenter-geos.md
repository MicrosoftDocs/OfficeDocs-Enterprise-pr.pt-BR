We recommend our customers to take no action, unless their organization needs core customer data to be stored at rest in their respective new datacenter geo. By choosing to move their data, customers limit Microsoft's possibilities to optimize the location of core customer data at rest in either their current or the new datacenter geo. As a customer in any of those two geos, you will experience the same quality of service, performance and security controls as you did before.</span></span>
